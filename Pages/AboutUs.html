About Recipe Finder

Recipe Finder is a website designed to help anyone discover, find, or even browse delicious recipes quickly and easily. You can browse recipes, search by ingredients or course type, and save your favorite recipes.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make cooking easier by providing a simple platform where people can find recipes that match the ingredients they have.

Website Features

Search recipes by name or ingredients
View detailed recipe instructions
Save favorite recipes
Admins can manage recipes
